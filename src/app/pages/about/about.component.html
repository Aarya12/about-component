At Jersey Bulk Shoes: sold and shipped from New Jersey, we bring you the highest quality footwear at even better prices. We take pride in manufacturing these with the highest standards of ethics with vegan materials that are ethically sourced.


OUR STORY

My feet ached. Not just physically, but with the weight of the world. Every time I'd slip on a cheap, trendy pair of shoes, I knew somewhere in the back of my mind what they cost. Not just the price tag, but the human cost. Underpaid workers, exploitive practices, a trail of waste that choked our planet. The online world was flooded with dropshipping deals, those "Made in China" labels flashing like warning signs. We all know the stories the exploitation, the injustice. The government fights, but the battle is far from over. Those faces haunted me.

We know that building something ethical requires hands-on approach, not relying on third party for their word. We are lucky that we get to craft these first hand and hire people to work with us just like we do and feel part of the family that is trying to save our planet. Our family gets to first-hand pick only the best vegan materials that are sourced ethically. Our slides and sandals are made from recycled materials, not animals. As an independently owned brand, we're committed to creating the future of sustainable footwear.
